Handwoven with Love Since 2018

Every piece at Wovencrafts is crafted by master artisans from the mountains of Afghanistan and the villages of Punjab, Pakistan. We use only the finest natural wool, pure silk, and vegetable dyes — no chemicals, no shortcuts.

From bold Afghani kilims that tell ancient stories to elegant Pakistani jaye namaz that bring peace to your prayers, each rug, cushion, and tote bag carries generations of tradition and passion.

We believe your home should feel warm, authentic, and full of soul. That’s why we ship worldwide with care and love.
